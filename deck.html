The Product
What We're Building
A subscription app delivering scientifically-calibrated awe experiences. Not meditation. Not breathing. You just watch and listen.
  • Moral beauty — real human kindness
  • Collective moments — shared human experiences
  • Nature — vast scenery and natural power
  • Cosmic — the scale of the universe
  • Music — engineered frisson
  • Personalized to each user over time
  • 90 seconds to 45 minutes
  • Every screen: phone, TV, VR
The opposite of doomscrolling.
10
Defensibility
The Moat
Layer 1
Scientific Partnership
Built with the researchers who discovered the science of awe. Exclusive co-branded clinical studies. Published in peer-reviewed journals. This locks out wellness-woo competitors.
Layer 2
Personalization Data
The app learns what produces awe in YOU specifically. Every session makes the next session better. The longer users stay, the harder they are to replace.
Layer 3
Biometric Dataset
Phase 2: Camera and Apple Watch measure your physiological awe response in real-time. We will own the only training corpus of what-produces-awe-in-whom. Future entrants can't catch up.
